# Talks

## Purpose
This page highlights conference talks and presentation material that are useful for understanding techniques, tradeoffs, and practical workflows in reverse engineering and security analysis.

## What to Include
- Talks that explain a concrete technique or analysis methodology.
- Presentations that provide useful context for recent tooling, malware analysis, or Windows internals topics.
- Materials that complement the repository’s patterns, recipes, and playbooks.

## Practical Guidance
- Favor talks that include examples, demonstrations, or enough technical detail to be applied later.
- Capture the central idea and any relevant caveats so the talk remains actionable even without the original video or slides.
