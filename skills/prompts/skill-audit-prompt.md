# Skill Audit Prompt

Review the entire <skill-name> skill and assess it against the repository's documentation standards.

## Review Goals
Verify that:
- Every knowledge document is complete and production-ready.
- Every pattern links to related knowledge or adjacent patterns.
- Every recipe references the relevant patterns and playbooks.
- Every playbook references the appropriate recipes and validation steps.
- Every example is consistent with the surrounding documentation.
- Cross-links are valid and useful.
- Placeholder, thin, or duplicate content has been reduced or removed.
- Naming follows the library conventions.
- Metadata is complete.
- Quality is comparable to the stronger skills in the library.

## Scope
Do not rewrite the framework or restructure the skill unless the change improves clarity or consistency. Focus on improving content quality, completeness, and maintainability.

## Review Workflow
1. Inventory the content categories in the skill.
2. Identify documents that are sparse, inconsistent, or missing practical guidance.
3. Improve the most relevant documents first.
4. Verify that the skill still flows coherently after the edits.

When the review is complete, summarize the improvements you made and call out any remaining gaps that should be addressed later.
