Quality Control Checklist

Before producing any final answer, run this checklist and record results.

Pre-output checks
- [ ] Observation provenance recorded for every cited fact.
- [ ] Evidence items scored and listed with independence noted.
- [ ] Hypotheses enumerated and ranked with numeric scores.
- [ ] At least one verification step executed for top hypothesis.
- [ ] Confidence stated with top contributing evidence and uncertainties.

Post-output checks
- [ ] Include rollback/mitigation steps for recommended actions.
- [ ] Provide a short error-analysis plan in case the conclusion proves incorrect.
- [ ] Attach linkable references to all tools/commands used during verification.

Automation suggestions
- Automate evidence scoring and provenance capture where feasible.
- Use templates for hypothesis reports to ensure consistency.
