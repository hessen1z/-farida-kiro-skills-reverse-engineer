Acceptance Criteria

Before merging any new skill or major change, ensure:
- It passes `tools/validate-skills.py` with no missing required keys.
- It is listed or referenced in `meta/` indexes (skills, knowledge-index, examples).
- It passes basic lint checks from `quality/lint.md`.
- A reviewer has recorded an approval or requested changes in `quality/review.md`.

If any step fails, attach remediation steps and reopen review after fixes.
