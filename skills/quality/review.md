Review Guidelines

Purpose: Outline human and automated reviewer responsibilities for new content.

Checklist
- Does the file state what it provides and prerequisites? (skills.json entry)
- Are examples present and runnable or clearly explained?
- Is evidence policy followed (provenance recorded)?
- Are verification steps included for claims?

Reviewer actions
- Approve, request changes, or reject. Provide clear actionable feedback.
