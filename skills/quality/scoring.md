Scoring and Quality Metrics

Purpose: Provide objective metrics for evaluating new content.

Metrics
- Completeness (0-5): presence of examples, references, verification steps.
- Reusability (0-5): clear inputs/outputs, machine-readable indexes.
- Testability (0-5): runnable examples or tests.
- Documentation (0-5): clear description, usage, and changelog.

Acceptance threshold: average >= 4.0
